About Us

The company


With over 25 years of experience in the field of IT and management software , Igesa offers IT solutions for small and medium-sized businesses, supermarkets, distribution centers, accountants and online stores.


The company also offers its customers an on-line and on-site help-desk service that carries out assistance, consultancy and support activities in warehouse management , accounting , billing and business intelligence , with the aim of guaranteeing solutions. able to respond to the specific needs of its customers.


In collaboration with its partners, Igesa offers its most demanding customers complete and integrated ERP solutions .
