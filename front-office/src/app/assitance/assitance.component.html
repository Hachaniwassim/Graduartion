Assistenza

Assistance on Igesa software is a fundamental sector for our company. In fact we are able to offer you an efficient service on the problems of our management software, thanks to the many years of experience of our operators.

Hotline Assistance and Customer Training Department: Telephone 0941-1935133 The Assistance Department of Igesa S.r.l. and deals with the coordination of the sector for all the technical-system aspects and customer support for software produced by the Company and the technical tests on all the software produced and marketed.

The Assistance Service is divided into: - Assistance on IGESA software (also in remote assistance) - Assistance on non-IGESA software: under this heading are the assistance services on software not developed by Igesa but on which a service is provided to customers. These include: - Multilingual telephone assistance (Italian, French, Arabic) - Customer training

For information and advice on the various services, contact the Assistance Manager at our offices. System Assistance System assistance is handled by Antonio Falconeri and includes customer assistance services on hardware and operating systems. It also provides for the configuration of the machines, installation and start-up of the Igesa software.
